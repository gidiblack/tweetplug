COST PER ACQUISITION (CPA) MARKETING

The services CPA marketing has to offer include:

Download – Cost per download (CPD):

With this service, advertisers can create ads for what they want their prospects to download. It can be an eBook, software, etc. They can also set the amount they want to pay per download to publishers.

Call – Cost per call:

If you have offers and want prospects to call you for more information or negotiations, you can create an ad and set the budget.

Signup – Cost per registration:

With this service, advertisers can create ads so that prospective customers can see and register or signup on their site.

Traffic – Cost per click:

Create ads so that visitors may see and click on it. This will increase your site traffic.
