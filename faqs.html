Frequently Asked Questions

I just subscribed, what next?

After subscribing, just login and click on your dashboard, you will see the 3 daily available sponsored content, you can either be asked to copy and paste or you can be asked to create your tweet and tweet it on your timeline, after tweeting, you are required to get a particular number of retweets on each tweet depending on the package you’re subscribed to then copy each link of each of the 3 tweets and paste it on your dashboard and click on SUBMIT LINK.

That is all!! You are done for the day. You will be credited with your revenue share before the end of the day if you did your work right.

Do I have to refer someone before I get paid?

NO, we do not have any referral program neither are we planning to introduce anything like that. If you trust us enough you can introduce Tweetplug as a source of income to your family and friends but we do not pay commission on that, we only pay you for promoting a business.

How long does withdrawal take after making a withdrawal request?

Withdrawal requests is every Sunday from 12am until 4pm into the bank account you registered with.

Withdrawals are processed within minutes but the maximum time frame is within 48hours.

Why am I allowed to tweet only 3 times in a day?

We do not encourage spamming at all, so all users are only allowed to tweet 3 sponsored adverts in a day.

Do I have to submit my tweet links daily?

It is absolutely necessary to submit your tweet links daily, because that is the only way we can confirm that you have completed your task and process your revenue share(payment).

What if I do not tweet sponsored ads in a day?

You will not get paid for that day. Please note that you will only get paid when you tweet and get the required number of retweets on each tweet. If you do not tweet, we will not get paid and we won’t be able to you.

What if I am not able to get my required retweets on each tweet?

We have a WhatsApp group to help customers with their required retweets on each tweet, after you subscribe you can request to join the group. Please follow the group rules in order not to be kicked out.

If you ever have a problem or want to make an enquiry, please call or message the Admin or any of the Frontmen and you will be attended to properly. If you complain and your problem does not get resolved, please send a mail to us describing your problem and include the name of the frontman you complained to. We have trained all our frontmen so anyone you contact should be able to assist and solve your problem.
